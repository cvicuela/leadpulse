# LeadPulse Data Moat Architecture

This document defines the long-term data advantage strategy of LeadPulse.

The goal is to create a defensible competitive advantage through proprietary data.

This module must not be implemented during the MVP stage.

---

# Vision

LeadPulse collects unique data about offline advertising performance.

Over time this data becomes a strategic asset.

The platform evolves into the primary analytics network for physical advertising.

---

# Types of Data Collected

The platform collects several types of data.

Scan Data

QR scans generated by physical ads.

Campaign Data

Information about advertisers, offers and industries.

Placement Data

Information about where ads appear.

Lead Data

Contact information generated from campaigns.

Conversion Data

Future integrations with CRM systems.

---

# Data Network Effect

As more campaigns run on LeadPulse:

More scan data is collected  
More lead data is generated  
More campaign performance insights emerge  

This improves the accuracy of analytics and predictions.

---

# Data Advantage

Competitors that only generate QR codes collect limited information.

LeadPulse collects full funnel data.

QR scan  
→ landing visit  
→ lead capture  
→ conversion

This creates a richer dataset.

---

# Predictive Analytics

Future AI models can analyze the dataset to:

predict conversion rates  
recommend campaign improvements  
suggest optimal placements

---

# Industry Insights

The platform may later provide industry benchmarks.

Example:

Real Estate Campaign Benchmark

Average Scan Rate: 8.3%  
Average Conversion Rate: 12.7%

These insights are valuable for advertisers.

---

# Marketplace Data Layer

The data moat enables a performance marketplace.

Advertisers can choose placements based on historical performance.

Publishers can optimize their advertising inventory.

---

# Competitive Advantage

Over time LeadPulse accumulates a unique dataset.

This dataset becomes difficult for competitors to replicate.

The more campaigns run on the platform, the stronger the data moat becomes.

---

# Implementation Rule

The data moat strategy depends on a large volume of campaigns.

Claude must not implement predictive analytics during the MVP stage.

The system must first collect sufficient data.
