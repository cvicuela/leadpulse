# LeadPulse SaaS Model

LeadPulse operates as a multi-tenant SaaS platform.

The platform supports three main customer types:

Publisher
Advertiser
Agency

---

# Core Value

LeadPulse converts offline advertising into measurable leads.

Example:

Magazine Ad
→ QR Scan
→ AI Landing Page
→ Lead Captured
→ Analytics Dashboard

---

# Revenue Streams

## SaaS Subscription

Publishers pay monthly for platform access.

Example pricing:

Starter: $99/month
Growth: $199/month
Enterprise: $399/month

---

## Campaign Fees

Optional monetization:

per campaign
per QR code
per lead captured

---

## Enterprise White Label

Large publishers can operate white-label instances.

Example:

ads.magazine.com

Powered by LeadPulse backend.
