# LeadPulse Growth Engine Architecture

This document describes the future growth layer of the LeadPulse platform.

It must NOT be implemented during the initial platform bootstrap.

Claude must only implement these systems after the core platform is stable.

Core platform requirements before implementing growth engine:

QR campaign engine operational  
Landing pages operational  
Lead capture operational  
Analytics dashboard operational

---

# Growth Engine Vision

LeadPulse evolves from a QR campaign platform into a **global offline advertising analytics network**.

The platform becomes the data layer for physical advertising.

---

# Growth Engine Modules

The growth engine contains four main systems.

1 Ad Performance Ranking Engine  
2 Publisher Marketplace  
3 Lead Quality Scoring  
4 Campaign Optimization AI

---

# Ad Performance Ranking Engine

LeadPulse will track campaign performance across publishers.

Metrics include:

scan volume  
lead conversion rate  
lead quality  
engagement time

The platform will generate performance rankings.

Example:

Top performing campaigns this week

This allows advertisers to choose high-performing publishers.

---

# Publisher Marketplace

Publishers can list advertising inventory inside LeadPulse.

Examples:

magazines  
billboards  
events  
tourism guides  
real estate signage

Advertisers can browse available inventory and launch campaigns.

Example marketplace listing:

Magazine Name  
Audience size  
Average scan rate  
Average conversion rate  
Price per campaign

---

# Lead Quality Scoring

Not all leads have the same value.

LeadPulse will create a scoring model based on:

engagement  
completion rate  
follow-up actions

Example score:

Lead Score 85/100

This helps advertisers prioritize high-value leads.

---

# Campaign Optimization AI

The platform will automatically optimize campaigns.

AI will analyze:

headline performance  
offer conversion rate  
landing page engagement

The system can suggest:

better headlines  
better CTAs  
better offers

---

# Network Effect

As more publishers join the platform, LeadPulse becomes more valuable.

The system creates a network of:

advertisers  
publishers  
campaign data

This creates a strong competitive moat.

---

# Implementation Rule

Claude must not implement these systems during the initial MVP build.

These systems are part of the long-term evolution of LeadPulse.

Only implement after core infrastructure is fully functional.
